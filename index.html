Welcome!

The 3rd annual Boğaziçi University Student Conference on Theoretical and Experimental Linguistics (BUSCTEL) will take place on February 22-23, 2020.

Keynote Speakers
Emrah Görgülü
Nihan Ketrez

BUSCTEL is an annual conference that includes all aspects of linguistic research. Once again, we hope to include presentations and posters by students from other departments, as well as other universities, and to create an environment in which students studying in the same field at different universities can exchange ideas and experiences.
